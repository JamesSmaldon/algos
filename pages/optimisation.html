Newton's Method (AKA The Newton-Raphson method)

A method for finding successively better approximations to roots of a real-valued function. Given an initial guess \(x_{0}\) and a function \(f\) with derivative \(f'\).

The Broyden-Fletcher-Goldfarb-Shanno Algorithm (BFGS)

This method approximates Newton's method Newtons method is centered around a quadratic approximation of \(f\) for points near \(x_{n}\).
